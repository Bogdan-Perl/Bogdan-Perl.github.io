Privacy Policy

For the Noble app · Effective date: July 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Noble app ("Noble", "we", "us") collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Noble you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Account information. When you create an account we receive your name, email address, and a unique account identifier from your chosen sign-in provider (Sign in with Apple or Google Sign-In). If you use Sign in with Apple's private email relay, we only ever see the relay address.
  • Learning data. Your progress through lessons and quizzes, answers and accuracy, streaks, XP, achievements, daily goals, and preferences you set (such as your theme, reminder time, and onboarding survey answers like your interests).
  • Subscription data. Your Noble Pro subscription status and purchase history. Payments are processed by Apple; we never receive or store your payment card details.

Noble does not include third-party advertising or tracking, and does not track you across other apps or websites.

How we use your information

  • To provide the app's core features and save your progress.
  • To sync your account and progress across your devices.
  • To personalize your experience (for example, surfacing lessons matching your interests).
  • To manage your Noble Pro subscription and entitlements.
  • To send the local reminder notifications you opt into (these are scheduled on your device; we do not operate a remote push server).

How your information is stored and shared

Your account and learning data are stored using Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Cloud Storage), operating on Google Cloud infrastructure. We share information only with the service providers needed to run Noble:

  • Google Firebase — data storage and authentication.
  • Apple and Google — to authenticate your sign-in.
  • Apple — to process subscription payments through the App Store.

We do not sell your personal information.

Deleting your account and data

You can permanently delete your account at any time from within the app: Profile → Delete Account. This removes your account and its associated data (your authentication record and your stored progress and preferences) and revokes the Sign in with Apple token where applicable. You may also email us at the address below to request deletion.

Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, the associated data is removed as described above.

Children

Noble is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
